Electric Vehicles

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Lower emmisions if electricity comes from green sources
  • Fewer maintenance means more savings and more envirmentally friendly
  • Quieter
  • With home charger always have a full charge when leaving the house
  • Take advantage of lower electricy rates by charging at off peak hours

Cons

  • Long Charge Times
  • Not many charger compared to gas stations
  • Battery loses charge over time
  • Higher electricity may cuase more electricy produced from fossil fuels
