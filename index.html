Self Driving

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Saved lives from reduced accidents
  • Increased efficency in travel
  • Reduced conjestion and traffic
  • More free time from not having to physically drive the car
  • Allow more access to travel for disabled
  • Significantly lower cost to travel
  • Potentially make extra money by allowing your self driving car become a taxi
  • Never worry about parking because car will always drop and pick you up
  • No longer have to worry about driving tests, car insurance, and driving tickets

Cons

  • Huge loss of high paying jobs
  • Accepting accidents caused by errors in self driving programs
  • Lose jobs supporting driving industry such as insurances and driving tests/lessons
  • Risk having more money accumilate in a few very rich and powerful companies
  • Car could get hacked and cause massive damage
